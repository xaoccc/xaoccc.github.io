Click The Boxes

Rules of the game:

Click the boxes with most of the same color around them. When you click on a box, the whole color sector resets and the total points of the color boxes from this sector add to your score. In 24 seconds the game resets. Try to score as much points as possible in 24 seconds

Yellow box score: 1 point

Orange box score: 3 points

Green box score: 5 points

Purple box score: 10 points
